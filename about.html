OUR STORY

From humble beginnings to global luxury icon

1985

The Beginning

Founded by master craftsman Robert Divine in a small Parisian workshop, our journey began with a simple vision: to create jewelry that tells stories.

1995

First Royal Commission

Our first royal commission established Divine Elegance as a trusted name among European aristocracy and luxury connoisseurs.

2005

Global Expansion

Opening our first international boutique in New York, marking the beginning of our global presence across five continents.

2015

Digital Revolution

Embracing technology while preserving tradition, we launched our digital presence to serve clients worldwide with the same personal touch.

2024

Present Day

Today, Divine Elegance stands as a symbol of luxury, innovation, and timeless elegance, continuing to set standards in the world of luxury goods.
